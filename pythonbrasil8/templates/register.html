Buy your ticket!

Corporate

If your company is paying for you to attend PythonBrasil, you should register at the corporate rate. You'll be helping to keep the conference affordable for all, especially for students and those needing financial aid. Government employees should also register at the corporate rate.

Individual

If you are paying for yourself to attend PythonBrasil, and you are not member of the APyB (Brazilian Python Association), feel free to come as an individual.

Student

This rate is for full-time students. You must present the documentation that proofs this condition in the first day of the conference.

Speaker

The selected speakers will be announced August 25, 2012. Therefore, if you are not an APyB member nor a student, we suggest you wait until this date, in order to buy your ticket.

APyB member

This rate is for members of the Brazilian Python Association (APyB), with the annual fee payed (R$ 40,00 for non-students per year). If in doubt, check the status of your membership.
